AML in a Day

A one-day intensive workshop designed to help your practice build a compliant AML/CTF program.

Details

When: Monday, 30 March 2026 | 8.30am – 5:00pm
Venue: BlackBay Lawyers - Level 30, 133 Castlereagh Street, Sydney NSW 2000
Facilitators: Holley Nethercote Lawyers, specialists in AML/CTF law and regulatory compliance.

What you'll walk away with

Every participant receives a template AML/CTF Program — including a Money Laundering and Terrorism Financing (ML/TF) risk assessment — that you'll begin customising during the day.

Topics covered include:

  • Governance structure
  • ML/TF risk assessment (with hands-on template work)
  • Personnel due diligence and training
  • Customer Due Diligence (CDD)
  • Reporting obligations
  • Program reviews and updates
  • Independent evaluations

You'll also receive access to Holley Nethercote's full suite of compliance tools, including registers, checklists, agendas and procedures. Approximately three weeks after the workshop, all participants are invited to a one-hour follow-up webinar to finalise their programs, get answers to any outstanding questions, and receive the latest updates from AUSTRAC.

VerifiMe will also configure your compliance software to support the key components of the program you develop on the day — so your technology and your obligations are aligned from the start.

Investment: $2,000 + GST per practice registration. We're pleased to offer two seats for the price of one — if you'd like to bring a colleague from your firm, both of you are welcome at no additional cost.

CPD points: Participants are entitled to 7 CPD units.

Register: Go to https://www.verifime.com/contactus#form and let us know you want to attend the workshop.
